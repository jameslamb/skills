Overview

Finding a new job is hard, and the internet is littered with listicles, clickbait, and shallow LinkedIn posts explaining the skills you have to have to progress in tech. Even if the quality of online resources were better, the problem remains...this stuff is hard! For example, can you get 10 of the questions from this app right?



In this project, we sought to build a tool that jobseekers can use to cut through the noise and get actionable recommendations for what to do next. Fundamentally, we tried to build software to answer this question:

I know [these things]. What should I learn next?

To learn more, visit the project Github repo.
