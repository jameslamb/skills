Square Product Manager, Point Of Sale

product manager, point of sale
the point of sale team is responsible for building and maintaining the ios and android apps that enable square sellers to accept payments and run their businesses. we are also responsible for onboarding across devices (including desktop). as a product manager for square point of sale, you will be responsible for driving product initiatives from inception through execution. in this role you will collaborate closely with designers, engineers, analysts, and other cross-functional team members within the organization to ensure that our products delight our sellers and grow with them as their businesses expand.
develop seller empathy to better plan and build products to meet their needs
create and maintain team's roadmap and milestones with strategic priorities in mind
define features, write product specs, facilitate user studies, iterate on feedback, help write test plans, manage the backlog, and ultimately ship in a timely manner
collaborate with engineering, design, and qa to foster strong team dynamics
ensure your team is prioritizing appropriately and synthesize trade-offs that must be made
represent the team in communication with cross functional teams
perform market research and analysis
experience shipping and iterating on successful products with cross functional teams
demonstrated leadership, and the ability to work well with designers and engineers
an analytical and metrics driven work style
excellent interpersonal, communication, organization and prioritization skills
prior experience at a consumer internet company, ideally in mobile. payments experience is a plus
7+ years of product management experience or equivalent
