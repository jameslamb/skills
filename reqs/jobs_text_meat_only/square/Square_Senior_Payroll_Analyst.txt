audit data integrity between workday hris and adp payroll to ensure both systems are in sync on a quarterly and annual basis
audit payrolls bi-weekly and semi monthly cycles before payrolls are released for processing
partner with payroll analysts on the payroll team in processing domestic payrolls
partner with internal and external business partners to test new functional system enhancements, system updates and integrations
lead year-end to ensure a smooth year-end to provide accurate w2's
collaborate with multiple business teams within the organization like hr, equity and business systems
identify and participate in process improvement projects, including various implementation and upgrades
monitor email inquiries from the payroll@ inbox and respond to employees as necessary
participate in payroll projects and opportunities as assigned
process garnishments, unemployment wage claims and other tax tracers as needed
work closely with the other payroll analysts to provide excellent customer service and be back up for payroll process as necessary
work closely with internal staff and vendors to ensure hr, benefit and payroll changes are processed, accurate funding occurs for all accounts, tax remittance, general ledger balancing , month-end close and management reporting is completed in a timely manner
assist global payroll manager with annual audits such as 401k and worker's comp
work with the team to provide guidance in payroll processes and provide feedback to improve payroll processes
