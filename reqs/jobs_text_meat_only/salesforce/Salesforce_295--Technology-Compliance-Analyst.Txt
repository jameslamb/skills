job title: technology compliance analyst location: san francisco, ca (hq)  role description: about salesforce: salesforce is the global leader in customer relationship management (crm) software. we pioneered the shift to cloud computing, and today we're delivering the next generation of social, mobile and cloud technologies that help companies revolutionize the way they sell, service, market and innovate-and become customer companies. we are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the world's most innovative company according to forbes and one of fortune's 100 best companies to work for. our more human, less corporate culture is built around doing work that matters, winning as a team and celebrating success aloha-style. department overview: the technology compliance (tc) team is part of a centralized risk, security and compliance (rsc) organization that reports to the vp of compliance and the evp, chief compliance officer. the tc function delivers technology risk management and compliance to support salesforce's #1 value of trust, protect our customers, and enable rapid growth. your impact: we are looking to hire an analyst with it audit, security, operations, and/or project management experience to help drive improvements within the tc team through the use of our common controls framework (ccf) and the solutions used to manage and deliver the ccf for our audit programs. key functions include understanding how multiple external compliance framework requirements map to our controls and improving the process for control owners, compliance team members and external audit partners. in addition, this role will help to automate the communication of control updates, audit requests and evidence collection through the use of our grc solution. as an important cross-functional role, other job functions may also include assisting with and/or driving the improvement of other key processes, projects, and pmo activities across the greater technology compliance team. while reporting into the office of technology compliance (otc) team, this role will also work closely with the external technology compliance (etc) team to understand the audit process and translate those requirements into workflows using the grc solution, our ccf, and internal ticketing systems. as a result of salesforce's on-demand application technologies and "software-as-a-service" business model, the tc team often confronts novel and challenging compliance issues. the team's goal is to support all aspects of the company's operations by partnering with the business to enhance key processes while enabling continuous compliance. the tc analyst must be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, interfacing with colleagues at all levels of the organization, and serving as an ambassador for the technology compliance programs throughout salesforce. roles and responsibilities
become a ccf expert by working closely with the otc and etc teams to own and drive improvement to our ccf-related processes, including the routine update to its data/content and the execution of our audit activities using our grc solution perform supporting external audit activities as appropriate, leveraging the ccf to update and maintain applicable audit documentation (including artifact request lists, or "arls”), manage evidence collection data, and update our control language partner with control owners, compliance teams and external partners to gather requirements and prioritize key process, content, and grc updates related to control, evidence, documentation, and self-assessment updates work closely with the otc team to carry out ccf-related updates in the form of managed projects that involve our people, processes and technology closely manage relationships with key team members, business partners, and supporting solution architects to carry out applicable responsibilities and keep projects on track develop innovative concepts, solutions, and improvements to increase the value-add and continuous maturation of the ccf program and the overall tc team
experience and qualifications

2-5 years of combined compliance, risk, and/or it operations, consulting, or security experience; supporting certifications a plus (e.g., cisa, crisc, cissp) strong written and verbal communication skills; ability to lead meetings and effectively communicate across all levels of the company analytical thinker who is highly organized, detail-oriented, and has the ability to work efficiently with minimal direction and/or oversight as well as part of multiple project teams simultaneously desired experience participating in security assurance examinations or audits with a working knowledge or understanding of compliance standards such as soc 1-2-3, iso 27001, pci dss, hipaa, and hitrust sufficient project management knowledge and experience either leading or playing a significant role on projects; direct grc implementation/development experience a plus ability to review business processes, identify points of improvements, and design solutions with limited/ambiguous data with minimal instructions ability to perform analysis on fragmented data and synthesize meaningful information; prior experience in data modeling a plus a "whatever it takes to get the job done" attitude (i.e., pick up the phone, stop by a desk, follow-up multiple times) with comfort working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment passion for our salesforce ohana, technology compliance, and delivering innovative programs that reinforce the company's focus on trust, as well as an eagerness to keep abreast with growing cyber security trends bs degree in management information systems, computer science, or a related field required; ms desired
