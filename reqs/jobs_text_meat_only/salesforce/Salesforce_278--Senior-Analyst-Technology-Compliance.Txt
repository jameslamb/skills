about salesforce: salesforce is the global leader in customer relationship management (crm) software. we pioneered the shift to cloud computing, and today we're delivering the next generation of social, mobile and cloud technologies that help companies revolutionize the way they sell, service, market and innovate–and become customer companies. we are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the world's most innovative company according to forbes and one of fortune's 100 best companies to work for. our more human, less corporate culture is built around doing work that matters, winning as a team and celebrating success aloha-style. department overview: the technology compliance (tc) team is part of a centralized compliance center organization that reports to the vp of compliance and the evp, chief compliance officer.  the tc function delivers technology risk management and compliance to support salesforce's #1 value of trust, protect our customers, and enable rapid growth. your impact: we are looking to hire an analyst/senior analyst with it audit and implementation, as well as, operations and m&a integration experience to help evolve and optimize our m&a compliance program within tc. this role will work closely with the m&a compliance team lead to mature the compliance posture of salesforce's acquisitions.  the role includes partnering with acquisition compliance contacts and internal business partners to drive compliance maturation efforts, evaluating technology controls across a broad set of assessments, and developing innovative means to deliver value to our stakeholders. additionally, the analyst/senior analyst will work closely with the m&a compliance team lead and external compliance team to coordinate audit readiness and drive remediation efforts within relevant acquisitions. as a result of salesforce's on-demand application technologies and "software-as-a-service" business model, the tc team often confronts novel and challenging compliance issues.  the team's goal is to support all aspects of the company's operations by partnering with the business to enhance key processes while enabling continuous compliance.  the senior analyst must be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, interfacing with colleagues at all levels of the organization, and serving as an ambassador for the m&a compliance, compliance evaluation process, and advisory programs throughout salesforce. roles and responsibilities

perform execution of m&a compliance initiatives including acquisition maturity assessments and advising acquisitions on tech certification efforts team with acquisition compliance contacts to understand their internal control environments, support their compliance objectives, and mature their individual compliance programs liaise with other tc programs to ensure acquisition compliance contacts are supported and have access to applicable materials and subject matter experts from across salesforce partner with teams across the business to provide guidance on technology compliance related questions and issues. proactively identify and communicate gaps in existing processes/controls and team with process/control owners to implement solutions to close the gaps assist process/control owners with the preparation and on-going maintenance of control documentation (e.g., policies, procedures, narratives) and deliver training when necessary manage relationships with external auditors and support their audit efforts/requests develop innovative concepts, activities, and solutions to increase the value-add and continuous maturation of the m&a compliance program and tc team

experience and qualifications

2-5 years of combined compliance, risk, it operations or security experience, with supporting certifications (e.g., cisa, crisc, cissp) bachelor of science degree in management information systems, computer science, or a related technical field required; experience in a compliance and regulatory role and experience with compliance standards across industries and geographies such as soc 1-2-3, iso 27001, sox, pci dss, fedramp, and hipaa experience developing, championing, and managing internal compliance programs a plus familiarity with multiple technology "backbones” and related supporting infrastructure analytical thinker who is highly organized and pays close attention to detail strong written and verbal communication skills; ability to effectively communicate and obtain buy-in at all levels of the organization and with internal stakeholders across the business ability to work efficiently with minimal direction and/or oversight as well as part of multiple project teams simultaneously a "whatever it takes to get the job done” attitude (i.e., pick up the phone, stop by a desk, follow-up multiple times) with comfort working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment passion for salesforce, technology compliance, and delivering innovative programs that reinforce the company's focus on trust
