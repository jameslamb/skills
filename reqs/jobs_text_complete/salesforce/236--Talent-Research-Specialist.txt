						 Title:  Talent Research Specialist About Salesforce:  Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the World's Most Innovative Company according to Forbes, and one of Fortune's 100 Best Companies to Work For six years running. The growth, innovation, and Aloha spirit of Salesforce are driven by our incredible employees who thrive on delivering success for our customers while also finding time to give back through our 1/1/1 model, which leverages 1% of our time, equity, and product to improve communities around the world. Salesforce is a team sport, and we play to win. Join us! Your Impact:  You are a curious person by nature and you thrive working with international teams. You are fascinated by technology and are excited to help Salesforce make data-driven recruiting decisions. You pride yourself on being organized, and often find yourself driving plans, projects, and life in general, forward. We are looking for an intellectually curious, self-motivated Talent Research Specialist to join our Recruiting Research team. Research is the foundation of passive sourcing and recruiting, and your responsibilities will be to help solve complex talent problems for the Recruiting Organization, Hiring Managers, and other key stakeholders through talent pool analysis and market research. By handling these challenging problems, you will help influence Salesforce's future growth strategy worldwide. Role Responsibilities:  
 Design and implement effective, results-driven research strategies and practices for recruiting research, talent mapping, and market intelligence Develop consultative business partnerships with Recruiting Leadership, Recruiters, Sourcers, and Business Leaders to address challenges and influence business decisions Extensively utilize proprietary and third party databases to identify high quality candidates based on business need Process research requests for Recruiters and Sourcers on an individual basis Provide regular, relevant technology industry updates to the Recruiting Team Identify new addressable markets, recruiting tools, and opportunities for finding top talent Proactively identify new opportunities to influence the business 
 Minimum Qualifications:  

 Bachelor's degree with 2 years of passive sourcing or qualitative research experience related to recruiting Experience leading multiple projects at a time, from conception to completion Extensive Boolean knowledge with the ability to craft accurate Boolean strings and assess search strings with a critical eye Ability to think globally, strategically, and objectively while effectively translating data into actionable insights and strategies Ability to shift direction quickly when priorities change to ensure the team successfully delivers on all deadlines flawlessly in a rapidly changing environment 
 Preferred Qualifications :

 Strong consulting skills with the demonstrated ability to successfully influence clients at all levels Enthusiastic and forward thinking attitude with the desire to improve the imperfect and come up with innovative solutions to business challenges Detail-oriented, self-motivated, and proactive with initiative Ability to synthesize information into concise, digestible content and break down, analyze, explain, and solve complex problems Excellent written and verbal communication skills Working knowledge of ATS/TMS reporting tools and technologies Proficient in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Google Apps
