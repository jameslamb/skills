Job Title: 
						 
						 Senior Product Manager, Search 
						 
					 
							 
								 
Job Category: 
								 
								 Product Management - Engineering 
								 
							 
							 
								 
Location: 
								 
								 US - California - San Francisco (HQ) 
								 
							 
							 
								 
SF-Y: 
								 
								 Pursuant to the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance and the Los Angeles Fair Chance Initiative for Hiring, Salesforce will consider for employment qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records. 
								 
							 

					 
						 
							 
							 
							 Share 
							 | 
							 
							 
							 
							 
							 | Email this job 
							 
							 
							 
						 
					 

					 
						 We’re looking for an exceptional product leader to join the Salesforce Search team and help revolutionize the way people use enterprise search. Search is critical at Salesforce; it’s the #1 most-used feature.  We help Salesforce users discover or retrieve the information they need in order to get the job done, over 100 million times a day.  Search at Salesforce is only getting more important as the company’s product portfolio and index grow. And with advances in artificial intelligence and machine learning, the work we’re doing is getting even more exciting and impactful.  Exciting times! Responsibilities:
 Drive the success of our Salesforce Search product Leverage market analysis, competitive understanding, and customer research (both your own and others) to define the roadmap for Search experience and bring the best of consumer web social and collaboration technology to the enterprise Translate product strategy into detailed requirements and then work with designers and developers to get it built, get it launched, and make our customers successful Bring a detailed eye for user experience and design to ensure features drive high adoption and customer enthusiasm Facilitate communication throughout the development process between a variety of internal teams as well as customer facing teams, ensuring that products satisfy customers’ needs and are highly adopted Serve as a product evangelist with Marketing, Sales, and the go-to-market organization to help them understand the vision, roadmap and best practices 
Desired Skills/Experience:

 2+ years of experience in Product Management 2+ years of experience working with Search at a company that does it really well Degree in computer science or closely related field Experience researching and analyzing user needs Excellent organizational skills, analytic capabilities, and attention to detail. Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority. Strong communication skills. Strong user experience/design sense 
Note: This role could be titled either higher or lower than Senior Product Manager depending on the experience of the candidate.
