Job Title: 
						 
						 Senior Full Stack Engineer, Gov Cloud Infrastructure 
						 
					 
							 
								 
Job Category: 
								 
								 Technology - Infrastructure Engineering 
								 
							 
							 
								 
Location: 
								 
								 US - California - San Francisco (HQ) US - Washington - Bellevue 
								 
							 
							 
								 
SF-Y: 
								 
								 Pursuant to the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance and the Los Angeles Fair Chance Initiative for Hiring, Salesforce will consider for employment qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records. 
								 
							 

					 
						 
							 
							 
							 Share 
							 | 
							 
							 
							 
							 
							 | Email this job 
							 
							 
							 
						 
					 

					 
						  
			   
						   
						       
						 Salesforce.com hosts web services and applications written by thousands of internal developers and tens of thousands of customers to provide the largest SaaS platform on the planet. Gov Cloud is Salesforce’s most innovative and secure offering for the Public Sector - helping customers meet their U.S. government compliance requirements. 

						 The Gov Cloud Infrastructure team works side by side with the Infrastructure Security team responsible for building the internal software platform that enhances the security posture of our production systems. Our expanding team is looking for a seasoned Distributed Systems Developer (position level dependent on experience). 
						 

						 Do you have a passion for security and interest in growing as a Cyber Security professional? Do you love agile coding, controlling hyperscale services and fully owning your service? Then this position is for you! 
						 
			 			 You will be building the new generation of Salesforce’s security platform, looking at security policy as part of service model, modeling policy control and implementing a hardened and scalable framework for enforcing policies. You will be responsible for designing, implementing and running our fleet of services that enforce the datacenter security posture. You will unlock security innovation and value by building a fully-automated platform with single-click policy management for tens of thousands of nodes, and services that self-heal and self-optimize.   
 

 Requirements: 

 
	 5+ years of development experience 
	 
	 Proficiency in at least one of the following programming languages: Golang, Java, C++, Python, C# 
	 
	 Proficiency in scripting languages like perl, bash 
	 

 
	 Demonstrated understanding of general Unix/Linux systems (e.g., CentOS, RHEL, Solaris, or similar) 
	 
	 Significant experience developing dynamic, scalable full stack web applications 
	 
	 Strong understanding of RESTful web service APIs and relational databases 
	 
	 Proficient with JavaScript and front-end frameworks like AngularJS 
	 
	 DevOps mindset and strong ownership over owned code (test, monitor, deploy, maintain) 
	 
	 M.Sc/M.Eng in Computer Science/Engineering or B.A/B.Sc. in same disciplines with the equivalent years of experience. 
	 
	 U.S. citizen (U.S. born or naturalized) who does not hold dual citizenship. You agree to complete a Minimum Background Investigation (MBI) for a Moderate Public Trust position with the U.S. federal government or other clearances as deemed appropriate for the role. 
	 
 

 Desired Skills/Experience: 

 
	 Cloud infrastructure experience in SaaS, PaaS, or IaaS 
	 
	 Educated in service operation technologies (Docker, VM, Puppet, AWS) 
	 
	 Excited by building reliable, self-healing services on unreliable hardware 
	 
	 Practical experience with pair-programming, agile development, test-driven development, and pattern-based coding (MVCS, CQRS, layered architectures) 
	 
	 Experience with implementing data visualizations, real-time monitoring, auditing or automation tools a plus 
	 
	 Active government security clearance preferred 
	 
 

 Company Overview: 

 Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. The company was founded on three disruptive ideas: a new technology model in cloud computing, a pay-as-you-go business model, and a new integrated corporate philanthropy model. These founding principles have taken our company to great heights, including being named one of Forbes’s “World’s Most Innovative Company” five years in a row and one of Fortune’s “100 Best Companies to Work For” eight years in a row. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, and this level of growth equals incredible opportunities to grow a career at Salesforce. Together, with our whole Ohana (Hawaiian for "family") made up of our employees, customers, partners and communities, we are working to improve the state of the world. *LI- Y
