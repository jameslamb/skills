Job Title: 
						 
						 Senior/Lead Software Engineer, Infrastructure 
						 
					 
							 
								 
Job Category: 
								 
								 Technology - Infrastructure Engineering 
								 
							 
							 
								 
Location: 
								 
								 US - California - San Francisco (HQ) 
								 
							 
							 
								 
SF-Y: 
								 
								 Pursuant to the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance and the Los Angeles Fair Chance Initiative for Hiring, Salesforce will consider for employment qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records. 
								 
							 

					 
						 
							 
							 
							 Share 
							 |

						 Senior Software Engineer Location: San Francisco, CA  We are looking for an experienced hands-on senior/lead software engineer to join a world class team of highly advanced systems and software engineers and infrastructure experts to deliver high quality automation frameworks to solve Salesforce’s top capacity and automation projects and initiatives. As Salesforce is pushing the limits in the IT world, whether in persistence or compute, we are above the current leading edge technology available in the market. As a Senior/Lead Software Engineer, you have a deep, working knowledge on infrastructure systems and will be responsible for leading key projects in design, development, and support of applications, and frameworks, that are used by a variety of internal teams at  salesforce.com .  You will work on complex scaling challenges as we move our customers to new infrastructures seamlessly.  The ideal candidate, an astute developer, will be able to learn quickly, work in a fast paced, team driven environment, is able to use his computer science knowledge and experience to solve problems by architecting and implementing solutions, while capable of communicating effectively with internal and external teams. Responsibilities: Provide technical and architectural leadership for the Infrastructure Delivery Engineering teams and Operations roles 

 
	 Design, implement and maintain the infrastructure used to power our service 
	 
	 Automate the deployment and instance migration process 
	 
	 Resolve complex technical issues and drive innovations that improve system availability, resilience and performance 
	 
	 Provide mentorship to other team members 
	 
	 Create, maintain and share technical documentation used by other team members 
	 

 Required Skills/Experience: 

 
	 Bachelor's Degree in CS or any equivalent engineering discipline 
	 
	 5-8+ years of infrastructure automation development experience 
	 
	 5+ years of design and implementation experience of large infrastructure projects with multiple teams 
	 
	 5+ years of in depth and hands on programming experience in Java, C++, C# 
	 
	 5+ years experience on scripting languages in at least one of the Perl, Python, Ruby, Go, JavaScript, Shell environments 
	 
	 5+ years of experience with Databases and SQL 
	 
	 5+ years of leading technical operation or system engineering teams 
	 
	 Strong analytical and problem solving skills in a project lifecycle 
	 
	 Strong understanding of Linux internals 
	 
	 Excellent written and verbal communication skills with outstanding attention to details 
	 
	 Experience in delivering engineering applications using agile/scrum methodology 
	 

 Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. The company was founded on three disruptive ideas: a new technology model in cloud computing, a pay-as-you-go business model, and a new integrated corporate philanthropy model. These founding principles have taken our company to great heights, including being named one of Forbes’s “World’s Most Innovative Company” five years in a row and one of Fortune’s “100 Best Companies to Work For” eight years in a row. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, and this level of growth equals incredible opportunities to grow a career at Salesforce. Together, with our whole Ohana (Hawaiian for "family") made up of our employees, customers, partners and communities, we are working to improve the state of the world. *LI-Y
