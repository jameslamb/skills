Job Title: 
						 
						 Manager, Corporate Customer Engagement & Marketing 
						 
					 
							 
								 
Job Category: 
								 
								 Marketing 
								 
							 
							 
								 
Location: 
								 
								 US - California - San Francisco (HQ) 
								 
							 
							 
								 
SF-Y:

						 If you’re a self-starter with a passion for innovative technology and customer success, including killer closing skills – we’re looking for YOU! The Customer Engagement & Marketing team is looking for a superstar marketer to support our corporate customer marketing programs. This individual will be responsible for managing our customer pipeline, researching customer accounts, understanding customer use cases, and aligning cross-functional product marketing managers to create the most relevant, impactful, and inspirational content. 

 The ideal candidate has strong interpersonal relationships-building  skills, creativity, and a can-do-attitude. The role requires a strategic thinker with the ability to sell customers the benefits to engage in joint marketing engagements. 

 Responsibilities will include developing and managing customer presentations and working with customers to secure commitment to participate in ongoing marketing activities (i.e. events, speaking engagements, etc.). 

 Role and Responsibilities 

 Research top customer accounts by aligning with product, industry and Sales teams Build a strong pipeline of customer leads to fuel our customer marketing programs Create customer presentations for joint-marketing opportunities Develop qualifying customer stories/references to leverage in various marketing materials Support company events by delivering customers to showcase top customer success stories Own flawless execution, track and measure customer success, and report weekly metrics Be an effective business partner and provide consistent and clear communication Build and maintain strong relationships with a diverse set of internal and partner constituencies, including creative, legal, finance, support, sales and marketing experts 

 Required Skills / Experience 

 Experience in developing innovative and compelling presentations Experience in launching new and innovative ideas with Experience with customer engagement; and/or agency experience Ability to work collaboratively across multiple business functions   3-5 years of experience in sales and marketing. Strong creative and relationship-building skills Passion for software-as-a-service, platform-as-a-service and disruptive thinking Excellent written, PowerPoint, and Apple Keynote skills Self-motivated individual capable of working in a face-paced, startup environment 

 Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the World’s Most Innovative Company according to Forbes, and one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work for, for six years running! The growth, innovation, and spirit of Salesforce are driven by our incredible employees who thrive on delivering success for our customers while also finding time to give back through our 1/1/1 model, which leverages 1% of our time, equity, and product to improve communities around the world. Join us!
