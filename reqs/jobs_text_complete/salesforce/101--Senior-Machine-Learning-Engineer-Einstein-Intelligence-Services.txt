Job Title: 
						 
						 Senior Machine Learning Engineer, Einstein Intelligence Services 
						 
					 
							 
								 
Job Category: 
								 
								 Technology - Software Development 
								 
							 
							 
								 
Location: 
								 
								 US - California - San Francisco (HQ) 
								 
							 
							 
								 
SF-Y: 
								 
								 Salesforce will consider for employment qualified applicants with criminal histories in a manner consistent with the requirements of the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance. 
								 
							 

					 
						 
							 
							 
							 Share 
							 | 
							 
							 
							 
							 
							 | Email this job 
							 
							 
							 
						 
					 

					 
						 Senior Machine Learning Engineer, Einstein Intelligence Services Role Description: The Intelligence Services team has an immediate opportunity for an extremely talented Senior Machine-Learning Engineer who wants to build products and features using machine learning and natural language processing to create a measurable difference for the company and create smarter, better products for all of Salesforce's customers. You will have access to an enormous amount of data that is very rich in signal, and includes high-value records of business activities, such as phone calls, meetings, calendar events, and full-text emails. It’s an opportunity to get exposure to modern data storage, messaging, and processing tools (Kafka, Cassandra, Amazon S3, Amazon EMR, etc.) You will be working with a group of world-class data scientists and data engineers to build intelligent services for both internal teams and customer-facing products. We are a small team with diverse technical backgrounds, growing quickly and working together closely. We share a passion for problem solving, new technologies, and learning. We love to to learn and teach and help each other, and we want someone with a similar attitude. 

 This role is based on Palo Alto, with the option to work from San Francisco two to three (2-3) days a week.   Technical requirements: 

 Industry experience building data pipelines and implementing machine learning models at scale Required expertise in Scala and the Apache Spark platform (including Spark Streaming, Datasets, MLlib, GraphX) Experience making consistency and availability tradeoffs Working knowledge of current techniques and approaches in machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) This is NOT a college graduate role, experienced candidates only please; minimum 3 years, preferably 5+ years PhD in Machine Learning and experience at well-regarded tech companies and startups are desirable 

 Personal Characteristics: 

 Self-sufficient: we operate like a startup, so everyone does a bit of everything to get things done Demonstrated ability to have a big influence is a must Creative thinking and a keen interest in brainstorming ideas for the next generation of data products A collaborative attitude and openness to others' ideas Prior experience providing services for internal teams to build customer applications You strive for excellence in everything, but can discern between the necessary and the nice to have
