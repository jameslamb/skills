 Job Title: Lead Systems Specialist - Salesforce on Salesforce Location: San Francisco 
 

 Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the World’s Most Innovative Company according to Forbes, and one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work for six years running. The growth, innovation, and Aloha spirit of Salesforce are driven by our incredible employees who thrive on delivering success for our customers while also finding time to give back through our 1/1/1 model, which leverages 1% of our time, equity, and product to improve communities around the world. Salesforce is a team sport, and we play to win. Join us! 
 

 Description: 

 The Platform Enablement team within the IT Enterprise Operations Organization focus on the internal success of Salesforce on Salesforce. The team collaborates with R&D leadership to evaluate new product features and champion the adoption within our internal Salesforce instances. As the Lead System Specialist, Salesforce on Salesforce, the person will be responsible for the operation of enabling new product features for the 3 times a year major releases and provide oversight of the adoption of emerging technologies on Einstein and Lightning platform. This individual contributor role will be a key contributor in the strategic Salesforce on Salesforce program which will enable Salesforce to showcase our internal usage of Salesforce platform on the latest technologies. 

 Responsibilities 

 
	 Establish and run the standard operations for assessing and enabling new product features 
	 
	 Establish process and tools to proactively monitor the usage of new product features 
	 
	 Maintain new feature enablement dashboard with progress and status update, leveraging tool such as SmartSheet 
	 
	 Maintain inventory of product feature gaps and target resolution date 
	 
	 Compile user adoption metrics for new product features through Einstein Analytics 
	 
	 Lead knowledge sharing and establish process for project hand-off and knowledge transfer 
	 
	 Manages project delivery in a fast-paced support environment, driving all issues to resolution with a strong focus on customer satisfaction and efficient task execution 
	 

 Required Skills 

 
	 4+ years of experiences as Salesforce program manager in large, complex environment (10,000+ seats) 
	 
	 10+ years of experiences as CRM analyst or project manager 
	 
	 Solid knowledge and experience of Salesforce products (Sales Cloud and Service Cloud) 
	 
	 Ability to coordinate with large cross functional business stakeholders 
	 
	 Experience with collaborating with Salesforce R&D groups to rollout new product features in a large complex customer org 
	 
	 Experience with communicating with business and R&D partners to prioritize demands for the team 
	 
	 Excellent verbal communication skills in articulating the functional and technical aspects of the new product feature 
	 
	 Champion of Salesforce IT Ohana culture with a focus of can do attitude 
	 

 Desired Skills 

 
	 Experiences with rollout out emerging technologies such as Lightning, Salesforce1, Wave, and Einstein 
	 
	 Experiences with managing the Salesforce platform in a multi-org environment 
	 
	 Experience in IT change management process 
	 
	 Experience in user change management 
	 
 Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. The company was founded on three disruptive ideas: a new technology model in cloud computing, a pay-as-you-go business model, and a new integrated corporate philanthropy model. These founding principles have taken our company to great heights, including being named one of Forbes’s “World’s Most Innovative Company” six years in a row and one of Fortune’s “100 Best Companies to Work For” nine years in a row. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, and this level of growth equals incredible opportunities to grow a career at Salesforce. Together, with our whole Ohana (Hawaiian for "family") made up of our employees, customers, partners and communities, we are working to improve the state of the world.
