 About Uber
 Uber is a technology company that is changing the way the world thinks about transportation. We are building technology people use everyday. Whether it's heading home from work, getting a meal delivered from a favorite restaurant, or a way to earn extra income, Uber is becoming part of the fabric of daily life. We're making cities safer, smarter, and more connected. And we're doing it at a global scale-energizing local economies and bringing opportunity to millions of people around the world. Uber's positive impact is tangible in the communities we operate in, and that drives us to keep moving forward.
  
 Summary
 We are seeking Account Specialists to guide interested people through the process of becoming a driver-partner with Uber! This is a great opportunity to work with a top-notch team on the daily functions that are at the core of Uber, and gain invaluable experience on the ground floor of a fast-growing, revolutionary organization. This job is full-time (40 hours per week), and some weeknights/weekends may be required.
  
 General Responsibilities
 Onboarding driver-partners onto our system by reviewing, maintaining and organizing driver-partner documentation
 Utilize online and offline sources to stay on top of Uber processes and tools
 Working collaboratively with peers and other departments to 
 streamline processes
 Build loyalty among new users
 Be a passionate advocate for riders and driver-partners
