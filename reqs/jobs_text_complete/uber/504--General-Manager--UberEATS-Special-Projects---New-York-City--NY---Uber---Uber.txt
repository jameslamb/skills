 General Manager, UberEATS Special Projects
 City Operations, Operations & Launch in New York City, NY
 Apply Now
  
 We’re changing the way people think about transportation. Not that long ago we were just an app to request premium black cars in a few metropolitan areas. Now we’re a part of the logistical fabric of more than 600 cities around the world. Whether it’s a ride, a sandwich, or a package, we use technology to give people what they want, when they want it.
  
 For the people who drive with Uber, our app represents a flexible new way to earn money. For cities, we help strengthen local economies, improve access to transportation, and make streets safer.
  
 And that’s just what we’re doing today. We’re thinking about the future, too. With teams working on autonomous trucking and self-driving cars, we’re in for the long haul. We’re reimagining how people and things move from one place to the next.
  
 The GM, Special Projects role is a unique, ground floor opportunity to lead the development of a new business segment within the UberEATS portfolio. You’ll be responsible for team building, strategy, process development, and ongoing management. We're looking for someone with the ideal mix of operational aptitude, the grit that's needed to persevere through uncertainty, the creative thinking needed to drive outside the box solutions, and the leadership abilities to manage a team in a fast changing environment. You can be based out of either San Francisco or New York.
 What You’ll Do
 Build out and directly lead a  team focused on a new business segment within the US & Canada UberEATS portfolio
 Oversee operations, sales, marketing, and data analytics activities for this business segment
 Help develop the develop the business strategy for this business segment
 Build a high performance team; motivate them to continually achieve higher levels of business impact
  
 What You’ll Need
 Minimum 8 years of deep, hands-on management of functions that include combinations of the following: operations, analytics, marketing, customer loyalty and retention, and project management. We will consider candidates with less experience if they meet our other requirements
 Sound business judgement and critical thinking skills, with the ability to use data to quickly establish highest value priorities and develop a strategy to pursue them
 Experience with quantitative data analysis, deep actionable insights, modeling, and advanced test plan design and analysis
 Positive energy and motivational leadership driven by personal influence and example with direct staff, peers, and senior leaders across all company functions
 Experience working closely and communicating effectively with internal and external stakeholders in an ever-changing, rapid growth environment with tight deadlines
 Significant experience managing teams
  
 Bonus Points If
 You've completed your MBA
  
 About the Team
 This is a newly formed team whose focus is developing a new business segment within the UberEATS portfolio. The team is based in San Francisco and New York City.
