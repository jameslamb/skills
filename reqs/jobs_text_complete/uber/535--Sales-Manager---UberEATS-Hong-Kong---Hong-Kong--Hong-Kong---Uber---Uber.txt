 Sales Manager - UberEATS Hong Kong
 Sales, Business & Sales in Hong Kong, Hong Kong
 Apply Now
 About Uber
  
 Uber is a technology company that is changing the way the world thinks about transportation. We are building technology people use everyday. Whether it's heading home from work, getting a meal delivered from a favorite restaurant, or a way to earn extra income, Uber is becoming part of the fabric of daily life.
  
 We're making cities safer, smarter, and more connected. And we're doing it at a global scale-energizing local economies and bringing opportunity to millions of people around the world.
 Uber's positive impact is tangible in the communities we operate in, and that drives us to keep moving forward.
 About the Role
  
 With UberEATS, we’re building Uber’s newest and fastest growing business, spanning 180+ cities across all six continents in two years. Today, UberEATS is available across many parts of Asia including Seoul, Tokyo, Taipei, Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur, Sydney, Perth, Brisbane, Adelaide, Melbourne, Mumbai and Bangalore -- and we’re just getting started.
  
 UberEATS is seeking a sales manager to lead our representatives in Hong Kong - someone who does this with both short and long-term market goals in mind, ensuring the right people and processes are in place to get there. You’ll work closely with general managers, sales operations, and marketing to support processes that scale and innovate UberEATS sales from the ground up.
 What you'll do
  
 Best-in-class sales: Drive your reps towards short and long-term market goals for Hong Kong, ensuring the right people and processes are in place to get there
 People management: Manage and cultivate highly successful and engaged reps who drive results. Monitor, evaluate, and develop the performance of individuals on your teams
 Team leadership: Create an incredible culture in your team centered on customer focus, passion for UberEats restaurants, and achieving the best possible results
 Local / regional collaboration: Facilitate focused and productive communication and collaboration among your reps and between your reps/cross-functional partners
 Stakeholder alignment: Collaborate closely with GMs and city operations to ensure your team is meeting the growth objectives of city leaders. Be a thought partner to your GM stakeholder on achieving restaurant growth in the region
  
 What you'll need
  
 4-7 years of experience in sales, marketing, lead generation, business development, or other relevant sales experience. Quota carrying experience preferred
 1-2 years attested leadership and staff development skills, including building and developing a sales team
 Exceptional communication and organizational skills
 Bilingual Cantonese and English fluency
