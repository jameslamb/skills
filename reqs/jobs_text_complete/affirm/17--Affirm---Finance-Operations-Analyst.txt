 We’re looking for intelligent, driven professionals to join our new Finance Operations team.  The group is responsible for supporting the Accounting, Finance and Capital Markets teams with core operational activities. The ideal candidate will have strong interpersonal skills while working cross functionally between teams and technical skills to back up their recommendations. 
 WHAT YOU'LL DO 
 Work closely with the Accounting and Finance teams to develop Looker dashboards and perform ad hoc data requests through Affirm’s loan platform
 Collaborate with Bank Engineering and Technical Program Managers to prioritize projects and identify areas of improvement to allow Affirm’s business operations to scale in a non-linear fashion
 Partner with Sales and Client Success on merchant reconciliations, promotions and accounting requirements for both Strategic Brands and Enterprise level accountsBuild the framework of Affirm’s treasury operations and identify areas of operational improvement
 Monitor and analyze accounting data and produce ad hoc financial reports or statements
 Improve systems and procedures and initiate corrective actions
 WHAT WE LOOK FOR 
 Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Economics, Finance or similar field
 1+ year of accounting or finance experience
 Experience writing and reviewing SQL Queries
 Nice to have: some knowledge of Looker, Python and git
 Ability to think creatively and critically and thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and often ambiguous work environment
 Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work in a team environment
 Enough laziness to find a faster way to do your least favorite things
